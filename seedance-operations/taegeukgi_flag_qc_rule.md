# Taegeukgi / 건곤감리 QC hard gate

Priority: highest / culturally sensitive.

For any visible Korean flag in source stills, Seedance uploads, generated video candidates, or final edit:

- Viewer-facing normal orientation must read:
  - upper-left: 건 Geon ☰ = three unbroken bars
  - lower-right: 곤 Gon ☷ = three broken bars / six short segments
  - upper-right: 감 Gam ☵ = broken / unbroken / broken
  - lower-left: 리 Ri ☲ = unbroken / broken / unbroken
- Taegeuk must be red over blue in a clean yin-yang swirl; do not invert colors or turn it into a generic circle.
- If the flag is folded, rippling, reflected, tiny, or partially occluded, it may be accepted only when no incorrect visible trigram is readable. A visibly wrong trigram or swapped corner is FAIL.
- Seedance cannot be trusted to fix a wrong source flag; wrong source stills must be regenerated/corrected with GPT Image2 before I2V.
- For I2V prompts on approved flag sources, include: preserve exact correct Taegeukgi geometry; do not invent, swap, mirror, rotate, duplicate, or simplify trigrams.
